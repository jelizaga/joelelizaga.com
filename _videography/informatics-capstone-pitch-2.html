Informatics Capstone Pitch #2

Director, editor, videography, sound.

TellToo

Precisely one quarter after producing our first capstone pitch video, our project had undergone at least four name changes and an aggressive UI development campaign; now, we were presenting TellToo. Despite the aesthetic changes to our branding and the name flipping, the concept remained the same: TellToo is a mobile-based storytelling social network based on temporarily accessible voice notes combined with pictures of the user's selection. You can read more about the project here.

This video continues the story-about-a-storytelling-app concept of the previous pitch. Each team member came up with stories tangential to one another's to plug into the app during the video, essentially demonstrating a use-case for the application.
