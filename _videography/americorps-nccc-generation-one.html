AmeriCorps NCCC: Generation One

Director, editor, videography, sound.

Generation One

This clip primarily focuses on the half of my team that worked with the children of the Generation One Academy in Houston, TX. Generation One is an after-school and preschool program for the underserved children of the Third Ward. They operate numerous other community service programs unrelated to the care of local children, such as free landscaping and home maintenance for the seniors and the elderly of the Third Ward, which is the half of the operation I worked with.

This is the second of the videos I produced for AmeriCorps NCCC as my team's media representative.
