Welcome to this first edition of Laico Lines Online, featuring highlights from the Laico Reunion of the Millennium. And what a fabulous reunion it was! Although Kathleen and I decided to enjoy a quiet Christmas at home, after spending every free moment building this site from piles of photos and Kodak Picture CDs and watching Malu's video tape repeatedly, I feel like I've lived the reunion over and over again for the past two weeks!

Thank you all for your contributions. I plan to mail a dual-platform CD-ROM of this Laico Lines edition with bigger video and sound clips to those of you who are interested. I will also continue to accept stories and pictures to post at this site throughout the year.

Andrew
