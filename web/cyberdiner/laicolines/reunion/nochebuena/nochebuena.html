Noche Buena at Chicho and Tonnette's

  

The Fruitful Generation

  
 

Chicho and Tonnette's house glittered like a Christmas wonderland. There were Christmas trees of all sizes and with different motifs -- angels, harlequins, gold baubles and bows, shimmering all over the house.

Empanada, enseimada, jamon and babingka -- Carol's pancit, Bingbing's pacit molo...

Pasko na, kain na!!
