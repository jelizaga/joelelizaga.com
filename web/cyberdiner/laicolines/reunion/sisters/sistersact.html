December 25, 1999

Sisters Act

Sporting matching "Sisters" T-shirts as their costume, Nini, Nonon, Bingbing, Mitos, Rory and Malu sashayed out "on stage" in a dance line to perform a routine to the song "Sisters." A sexy hot-pink boa was very effectively used as a prop. Every one of the sisters grinned unabashedly as she effortlessly performed the moves with Laico trademark grace and charm. Everyone had to laugh at the lyrics, so appropriate, but ended comically with:

Lord help the mister

who comes between me and my sister

And Lord help the sister

who comes between me and my man!

I later asked Nini how she came up with the song, it seemed to fit so well. She said, "Oh, Ching and I used to sing that all the time, and something just recently reminded me of it. The only thing was, I couldn't remember the title or where it came from, and I asked in all the music shops and couldn't seem to find it. Then I mentioned it to an old friend of mine and she said, 'Oh, it's in Bing Crosby's White Christmas!' Well, you know how hard it is to find anything in Morton, but there it was in the video rentals."

And the rest is (Laico) history.

 

 

 

After the Sister Act number the audience yelled, "More, more!" After they actually repeated the dance, Salman yelled, "No more! No more!"
