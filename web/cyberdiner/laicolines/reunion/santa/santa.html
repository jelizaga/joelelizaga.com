Seeing Santa

  
  "Close your eyes!", Malu says before Santa arrives.

 

 
 

Rahim looked at Santa and declared, "You're not Santa!"

Santa replied, "But I could be one of Santa's helpers though, and that's the more important thing."

 

 
 

Only Santa could make shy Jason speak a word in public: "Pikachu!"

Isabelle Dizon started crying when she heard Santa's voice. She clung ferociously to her Dad and got a present for doing so. Later while talking to Paolo:

Isabelle: "Where did the big Santa go?"

Paolo: "I don't know. The North Pole?"

Isabelle: "Is he coming back?"

Paolo: "Maybe next year."

Isabelle: "Okay."

...and she went back to playing.

Popoy's neighbors were delighted to see Santa on his way to Popoy's house, and said, "Santa, come on over to our house and see the kids!"

Ten-year-old Greg to Paolo: "Aha! I knew it was you!"

Paolo: "What are you talking about?"

Greg: "Weren't you Santa?"

Paolo: "Of couse not, I was standing over there."

Greg: "Oh. Duh."
