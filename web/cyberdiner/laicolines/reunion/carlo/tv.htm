Caluykuy's TV Corner

Can't be called an "Entertainment Center" because it lacks a VCR, Hi-Fi, and a PlayStation. New Year celebrations at Red Square happened to be on TV when this shot was taken. South Park's "Cartman" on top of the TV. TV, in turn, is on top of the corner table. That thing behind it is the ultra-low tech Air Conditioner/Heater. No timers, no vents strategically positioned around the studio, and I hardly use it because it will make the TV overheat.
"TOUCH THE SCREEN!": Am I getting too gullible? Is this televangelist getting to me? Cartman now joined by a larger-scale Kenny.

NOTE: If you're wondering about the location of the TV, the bedroom area is to the right (corner of bed is visible). I rotate the TV depending on where I'm watching -- on the sofa, at the dinette while eating dinner, or in bed before sleeping.
