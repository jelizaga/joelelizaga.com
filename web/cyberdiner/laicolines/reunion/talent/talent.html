December 25, 1999

Among the Talents...

Ricky Martin (Elizaga) and Carlo Santayana

  
 

Carlo, Alfie, Aimee and Gina singing Laico No. 5

  

 

The reunion proved a great opportunity for new talents to shine:

 

Laico No. 5 was an impromptu song put together by Jeff, Alfie, Carlo and Paolo about their obsession with Filipino food, while Laico Blues was another last-minute song written by Jeff and Jorge.

Never underestimate a Ricky, especially our own Ricky Martin (Elizaga), webpage maker by day and karaoke singer by night.

Isa Ford played her role as the Virgin Mary seriously and stayed in character like a real actress. Later she danced and twirled around to everyone's delight.

Eamon Ford stunned the audience by saying the "Our Father" in LATIN! It's hard enough for some people to remember it in English.

Malu has a really smooth voice. She'll be the wedding singer for the next couple to be married.

Carlo Santayana can kneel and play the guitar at the same time but he can also bake apple pies while Carlos Santana cannot.

Paolo's still got showmanship although he's been out of the a capella group Bostonians for a couple of years.

Cris, Sondra, Marnie, Aisa, Lia and Carol showed imagination and resourcesulness using a Christmas tree skirt, bedsheet, and foil wrapper for the kings' capes, and Burger King crowns to create the kids' pageant costumes.

Aimee and Nelson can definitely go pro and win a lot more bowling tournaments.

  
  Greg's violin playing showed some beginnings of an Itzak Perlman.
