The Polvoron Eating Contest

  
 

Pity Mike Ford, polvoron-eating contestant: "It's the most awful thing I've ever eaten. I'll never eat it again. It's like eating sawdust mixed with glue."

We hear you Mike. Next time it will be a balut-eating contest.

  
   
   
   
   
   
 

Like we didn't notice you:

Salman, polvoron-eating contest winner, had the advantage of watching all the other contestants try to eat polvoron and whistle at the same time. Since he was the last contestant, he figured out the trick and even beat polvoron Popoy.
