Film Actress Files Charges Vs. Estela

Movie actress Lucita Soriano filed charges of slander and grave physical injuries yesterday against fellow actress Estela Suarez at the Las Pinas municipal court after Miss Soriano was hit and wounded by a glass bowl allegedly thrown at her by Miss Suarez.

The victim, who is now confined at the St. Luke's hospital, suffered wounds on her face including her left eye.

According to the police, Miss Soriano was on a location shooting at the Palm Groove beach resort the other day when Miss Suarez arrived at the scene with another movie actor.

Minutes later, police said, the two women exchanged heated words which ended in the glass throwing incident.

Dr. Jaime Laico of the St. Luke's hospital said last night that Miss Soriano was in good condition. At the same time, the victim's younger sister, Nenita, told The Bulletin that reports regarding her sister's getting blind were not true.

She said her sister was feeling well despite the injuries she suffered.

At press time last night, Miss Suarez was still at large.
