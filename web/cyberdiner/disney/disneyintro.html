Walt Disney World is probably the most photographed site on earth, both on and off the Web. If you don't believe it, just search for "Disney". While we at the Elizaga CyberDiner share with you our Disney World trip, we choose to add something different than what you might expect. Of course the E-rides are still our favorite and deserve a few words, but we also want to list for you some of the best of the underappreciated, underrated sights of Disney World - the stuff hardly mentioned in the official (and unofficial) guides, but exemplary of the attention to detail that helps make Disney magic.
