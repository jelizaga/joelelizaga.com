University of Washington

The University of Washington is a university located in the state of Washington. The humans congregate here to drink brown water and discuss things. There's a large toilet on campus with an disability-accessible ramp for crippled ducks and ducklings. Around summertime the geese will invade campus. Don't trust the seagulls. The seagulls are thieves.
